Movie Summary

The Shawshank Redemption follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of the murder of his wife and her lover. As he adapts to life in Shawshank prison, Andy forms a strong bond with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding, ultimately leading to an inspiring escape and a quest for freedom.

Actors

Tim Robbins plays the role of Andy Dufresne, a man who finds hope and redemption through his unwavering perseverance and intelligence.

Morgan Freeman portrays Ellis "Red" Redding, a lifer at Shawshank who becomes Andy's closest friend and a symbol of hope in the harsh prison environment.

Favorite Quote

"Get busy living, or get busy dying." - Andy Dufresne

This quote encapsulates the theme of hope and perseverance, reminding us that we always have a choice in how we face life's challenges.
